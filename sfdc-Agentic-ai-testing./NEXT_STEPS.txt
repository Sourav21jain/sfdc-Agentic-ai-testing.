│ STEP 3: CONFIGURE THE AGENT (5 minutes)                          │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Commands:
  cd /Users/soujain/insights-tool
  cp config/.env.example config/.env
  nano config/.env

Fill in:
  SFDC_USERNAME=your.username@example.com
  SFDC_PASSWORD=YourPassword
  SFDC_SECURITY_TOKEN=TokenFromEmail
  SFDC_DOMAIN=test
  ANTHROPIC_API_KEY=sk-ant-your-key
  LOG_LEVEL=INFO
